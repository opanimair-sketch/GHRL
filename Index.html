About Us

GHRL Infra Engineering Pvt Ltd

Established in 2023, GHRL Infra Engineering Pvt Ltd has quickly become a trusted name in construction equipment hiring. Although we are a relatively new company, our team brings years of technical expertise and industry knowledge.

We specialize in providing complete solutions for hiring construction equipment including batching plants, concrete pumps, boom placers, and transit mixtures.

With our own fully equipped workshop near Mundka Industrial Area, Delhi, we ensure all our equipment is maintained to the highest standards by our trained hydraulic mechanical team.
